Serverless usage patterns

The ways to utilize serverless technology is limited to only the imagination of the design team involved in the system creation. Every day new techniques are developed that further expand the designs of what can be achieved with serverless, and due to the fast pace of innovation by the cloud providers, the new features and services in this area are increasing all the time. However, with multiple years of maturity at this point, a few core serverless design patterns have emerged, namely web and backend app processing, data and batch processing, and system automation.
