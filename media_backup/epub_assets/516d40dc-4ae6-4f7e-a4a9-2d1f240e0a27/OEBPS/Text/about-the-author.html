about the author

Morten Barklund holds a Master of Science degree in Computer Science and boasts more than two decades of experience in frontend web development. Currently, he is a staff engineer at Corti, a pioneering medical AI startup. Beyond his day job, Morten is deeply involved in the tech community, hosting a local TypeScript meetup, speaking at various conferences, and teaching workshops. This book is Morten’s third publication, reflecting his dedication to sharing knowledge and fostering the development of fellow web professionals through practical insights and expert guidance.
