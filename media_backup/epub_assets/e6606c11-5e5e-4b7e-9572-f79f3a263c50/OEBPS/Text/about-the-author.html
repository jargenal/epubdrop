about the author

Elton Stoneman is a Docker Captain, a multiyear Microsoft MVP, and the author of more than 20 online training courses with Pluralsight. He spent most of his career as a consultant in the .NET space, designing and delivering large enterprise systems. Then, he fell for containers and joined Docker, where he worked for three furiously busy and hugely fun years. Now he works as a freelance consultant and trainer, helping organizations at all stages of their cloud and container journeys. Elton writes about Docker and Kubernetes at https://blog.sixeyed.com and on X @EltonStoneman.
